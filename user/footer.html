Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Data Collection

Dream Makers Youth & Sports Club (DMYSC) collects personal information necessary for membership management. This includes your Full Name, NIC Number, Date of Birth, Address, Contact Number (WhatsApp), and Profile Picture. We also collect authentication data (Email/Google ID) and payment history.

2. Purpose of Use

Your data is used to:

  • Verify your identity and eligibility for club membership.
  • Generate your Digital Member ID card.
  • Track monthly fee payments and issue receipts.
  • Coordinate sports teams and event participation.

3. Data Security

We utilize industry-standard encryption (via Supabase) to protect your personal data. Your password and sensitive session tokens are never exposed. We do not share your personal details with third parties for marketing purposes.

Effective Date: January 2026

1. Membership Agreement

By registering on the DMYSC Digital Platform, you agree to abide by the constitution and rules of the Dream Makers Youth & Sports Club. Membership is subject to approval by the executive committee.

2. Financial Responsibility

Members are required to pay a monthly membership fee of 100 LKR. This platform tracks your payment history. Failure to settle dues may result in the suspension of voting rights or membership privileges.

3. User Conduct

You agree to use this platform responsibly. Any attempt to manipulate the digital ID system, falsify payment records, or harass other members through the platform will result in immediate termination of your account.

4. Account Security

You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your login credentials. If you suspect unauthorized access to your account, please notify the administration immediately.
